EZ Mount

New from ProControll is the EZ Mount universal bracket that can be mounted virtually anywhere on a boat for use with most makes and models of transom mount trolling motors

Project Details

  • Universal Mount - Will mount to the bow or side railing of a utility or jon boat with a gunwale or top rail width of up to 3 inches.
  • Simple to install and use - Mounts as easily as the motor itself. No tools required for installation.
  • Convenience – Maintains all the functionality and convenience of the trolling motor.
  • Built to last in harsh marine environments – All metal parts are Hardened Stainless Steel or Zinc Plated for years of trouble-free operation. External parts are made of rust-proof, virtually indestructible Fiberglass Reinforced Nylon.
